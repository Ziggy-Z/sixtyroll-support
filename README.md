## About SixtyRoll

SixtyRoll helps participants coordinate **synchronized race starts** using real-time speed matching.

The app is intended for use **only on closed tracks or private courses**.  
Do **not** use SixtyRoll on public roads or in traffic.

---

## Privacy & Safety

SixtyRoll is committed to user safety and responsible use.

Any misuse of the app or behavior that violates safety guidelines or applicable laws may result in restricted access.

If you encounter a safety issue or believe the app is being misused, please report it immediately using the support email above.
